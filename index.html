About Us

Tired of NFTs being minted too fast? Are you sick of waiting around all day to click a button?

  • We plan to launch in October with a starting mint price of .05 ETH
  • 10,000 unique NFT's every season
  • More than just a click of a button

What The Fish aims to fix this with a unique approach to minting. Rather than clicking a button, anyone who wants to mint of our our unique fish will need to sail around Lake Minnow and find one. We're building a system that will slow down the minting process so you can kick back and fish at your leisure during fishing season.


How it works

Once you’re onboard your ship you will need to navigate the harsh waters of (Insert Lake Name) in search of fish. The only way to mint these NFTs is searching for groups of fish and dropping your lures. In game you will be able to select which lure you prefer. Choose your lure wisely, it will determine the rarity of your fish (or Mermaid?). Keep catching and collecting those fish! Once you reach certain milestones in your collection, you’ll get new and fin-proved vessels! We can’t guarantee they will improve your e-fish-iency, but they do look pretty sick.

We have some pretty strict catch-and-keep policies here. There will be limited quantities of fish that will replenish on a timed basis. Since you won’t be able to sit around all day catching fish you won’t have to worry about getting carp-al-tunnel from this game

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I mint a Fish?

    The only way to mint a fish is to play our game during the minting window. You will need to sail around Lake Minnow to find fish to catch.

  • How much will it cost to mint?

    Our intention is to start the minting price at 0.05 ETH

  • When can I start fishing?

    We are wrapping up the development of the game and contract. As we iron out the last few details and features, we are building a community. We are planning to open our first fishing season in late October.

  • Why take this approach to minting?

    A lot of new NFT projects require you to be present at the time of mint for a chance to mint an NFT for yourself. Sometimes this happens during high traffic times forcing everyone to pay huge gas fees. Anyone who wants to purchase at mint price for a hyped mint has to eat the gas. What we're building staggers the mint so it can happen over time, allowing people to get in when they can and mint during low traffic times to avoid outrageous gas fees.
